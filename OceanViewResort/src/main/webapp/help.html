Quick Start Guide

01

Login to the System

Navigate to the login page and enter your assigned staff credentials provided by the IT department.

02

Add a Reservation

Click "New Reservation" on the dashboard or sidebar. Fill in the guest name, room type, and dates. Click Save.

03

Generate a Bill

Search for a reservation, open the detail view, and click "Generate Bill" to compute total stay cost automatically.
